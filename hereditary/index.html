horror today is the shitfaced brother of the genre that used to depict dread, hinged on cramming as many jump scare shots into a meter of movie film as possible. we do not get to empathize with a character anymore. we don't get to be scared with them. characters became plot devices and the horror an element of surprise. isn't the whole point of a scary film to inflict fear before anything happens on screen? hereditary made me realise that not all hope is lost
