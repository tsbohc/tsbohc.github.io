side-effects of affection

my normal self is derailed the moment i realise i am attracted to the person next to me. in a heartbeat gone is the peace of mind, and my thoughts spiral into the dismaying fear of the aftermath

love is honesty and acceptance that prevail against the fear of lack thereof. if you have feelings for someone, it’s natural to fear rejection; the issue is, everything you do or say will be filtered through that fear

no matter how harsh it may sound, there’re no irreplaceable people. there’re people who are excruciatingly painful to replace. but that’s life, and what doesn't kill you makes you stronger. don’t let fears taint your being and for the love everything, don’t be afraid to be yourself
