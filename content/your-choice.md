intentionally naïve is how i often describe myself. it's not an act of deception, but an excuse to never part with that childish self i once was. do you recall that feeling? when existence itself was a novelty? it used to accompany every moment of your life and now is but a shadow you don't have the time to chase

your capacity to feel alive has become grounded in the weight of responsibilities, dulled by experience and cold rationality. and all you got in return is one frail sensation and a cracked rose-coloured lens of nostalgia

but you don't have to accept that. the world itself hasn't changed. the cycle of monotony is within you. break it. stop taking things for granted. learn to appreciate the simple gifts given to you every day. life is an adventure. if it's not, make it one
