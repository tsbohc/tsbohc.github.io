the side-effects of reading may include: alteration of speech patterns, excessive use of metaphors, uncannily vivid perception. proceed with caution. jokes aside,

think of literature as a manual for the subconscious to attune the brain to a certain frequency. books are designed to stir up the imagination, and some of those images are bound to be cast into memory

a reading mind indulges in the intricacy of writing, builds up the emotional vocabulary, and cultivates the ability to express until every thought becomes a poem within of itself. the dream, isn't it?

> the cat breathing sleeping like the ocean waves(soft to touch) reaches for the moon down the panes of frozen glass, hell. i’ve been reading ee cummings, can you tell?
