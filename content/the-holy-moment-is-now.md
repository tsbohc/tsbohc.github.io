having grown up in an orthodox family, religion was never forced onto me. christianity taught me to love and forgive, and that alone outshone the need to doubt or prove the origin of its teaching

i turned agnostic and interpreted religion as a philosophy, but to accept that the phenomena of life occurred because some variables happened to match -- that's just too undeniably sad

i'd rather believe the tale that the world is a work of art, an expression of will ushered into existence by the Lord's hands
