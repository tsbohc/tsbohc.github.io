the pattern becomes more intricate and subtle, yet some insist on living in denial of agency out sheer convenience. a victim of circumstances cannot really be held responsible. they have already belittled themselves into losing control over their own life

it's a price payed in blood by those who have adopted ignorance as their life's philosophy. those who have dethroned reason to bestow a crown upon feelings. those who fail to see the distinction between love and desire. those who are afraid to lose things they've never truly had

you mourn the lack of joy in existence when your coffin has already been nailed with lies, buried beneath pretension, fallacies, self-interest. by yourself. wake up and smell the coffee. it's time you took responsibility for every breath you've taken and every feeling you have held
