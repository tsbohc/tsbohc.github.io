ex vinum, the post mortem

I wrote ex vinum because I'd never played a table-top rpg, but I was autistic enough to already want to create one. Also, my girlfriend said she wanted to play D&D with me.

And that's when all justifications fade into the sweet nothingness of love. I wanted her to have fun, the rest didn't matter.

Yet, there was a hunch. She had no prior experience with the genre. I turned to the D&D starter set, and even that seemed too daunting, not to mention the player's handbook.

I got down to reading, adapting the rules and mechanics. The spreadsheet quickly grew out of control. A few weeks later, I began learning LaTeX and moving everything to a proper format.

And that's how ex vinum was born, a 10 page rulebook in two languages.
