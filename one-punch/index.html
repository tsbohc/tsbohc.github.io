one punch

stuff like this isn’t usually my cup of tea so i was late to jump on the one punch hype train, but i’m glad i did

one punch man is a show that does many things right. it knows exactly what it is and doesn’t try to be anything else, yet still manages to surprise the viewer with words of wisdom that fit ever so perfectly into our hero’s character

saitama is a great hero not because of his immense strength and ability to kill everything in a single punch, but rather because of his motives and beliefs

“i’m just a guy who’s a hero for fun”, he tells everyone

that’s not entirely true. he doesn’t compete for higher rankings, public appeal or popularity. he’s an altruist and a humble one for that matter. he’s willing to go full out to save lives. his actions are selfless, his motivations are pure. a single plain thank-you letter is all it takes to turn him speechless

yet he gets a lot of flak for his actions and people are constantly trying to leave him without any credit. some are bold enough to blame him for not doing a better job at being a hero. the show’s crowd is so realistically dumb

one has to be pretty cold-hearted to feel nothing towards the mc. i’d be lying if i said i didn’t want to shed a tear once or twice or hell, a bunch of times throughout the show. i don’t know, it just pushes the right keys for me

the animation may be a blend of a dozen of visual styles but is still awesome and far above average. and from what i’ve seen it stays true to the manga, which more often than not is a good thing. the fights are flashy and fun to watch. nothing to throw tomatoes at, if you ask me. especially that “ok” face. my god i laughed

the soundtrack ties everything together nicely and sets the right mood and is nothing to complain about either

wrapping up, i could find things to criticise the show for if i’d wanted to. but you know what? i don’t

just go watch it. have a good time
